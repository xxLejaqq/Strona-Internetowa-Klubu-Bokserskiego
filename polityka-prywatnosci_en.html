§3 Data Retention Period

1. Personal data will be stored for the period necessary to handle correspondence and provide an answer, but no longer than 12 months from the end of contact, unless legal provisions impose an obligation for longer storage.

§4 User Rights

The user has the right to:

  • Access their data and receive a copy of it.
  • Rectify (correct) data.
  • Erase data ("the right to be forgotten").
  • Restrict processing or object to processing.
  • Lodge a complaint with the President of the Personal Data Protection Office.

§5 Cookies

1. The website uses cookies, which are IT data stored on the User's terminal device.

2. Cookies are used for statistical purposes (e.g., Google Analytics) and to ensure the proper functioning of the website's features.

3. The user can change the cookie settings in their web browser at any time.

4. Detailed information about cookies can be found in the Cookie Policy tab.

§6 Data Sharing

1. Users' personal data is not sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

2. Data recipients may only be authorized state authorities based on legal provisions or technical service providers (hosting, email) exclusively to the extent necessary for the functioning of the Website.
